Quick Tutorial

  1. Enter text: Type your text into the text area provided.
  2. Select language and voice: Choose your preferred language and voice from the dropdown menus.
  3. Convert text to speech: Click the "Convert to Speech" button to generate the audio.
  4. Listen and download: Use the "Play" button to listen to the speech, and click the "Download MP3" button to save the audio file.
